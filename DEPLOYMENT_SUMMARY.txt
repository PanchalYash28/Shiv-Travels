                           📋 API ENDPOINTS

Registration (Email OTP):
  POST /api/auth/send-email-otp         → Send 6-digit OTP to email
  POST /api/auth/verify-email-otp       → Verify OTP & register account

Google Authenticator Setup:
  POST /api/auth/setup-totp             → Generate secret & QR code
  POST /api/auth/verify-totp-setup      → Enable TOTP with verification

Login (Flexible Choice):
  POST /api/auth/check-auth             → Check available auth methods
  POST /api/auth/send-login-otp         → Send login OTP email
  POST /api/auth/verify-login-otp       → Verify email OTP & login
  POST /api/auth/verify-totp-login      → Login with Google Authenticator

Management:
  POST /api/auth/disable-totp           → Turn off Google Authenticator
